Rules of the Game

  1. First Enter a Number in the text box on top and click the arrow which will be the Target to win the game
  2. The Level entered MUST BE any number above 10
  3. The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  4. In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND score
  5. BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
  6. The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his Total score. After that, it's the next player's turn
  7. The first player to reach the Selected Level points on the Total score wins the game
